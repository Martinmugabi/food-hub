Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem a FoodHub gift card?

You can redeem your gift card during checkout on our website or mobile app. Simply enter the gift card code in the payment section, and the amount will be applied to your order.

Do gift cards expire?

No, FoodHub gift cards never expire. The recipient can use them whenever they're ready to enjoy our services.

Can I use multiple gift cards on one order?

Yes, you can apply multiple gift cards to a single order during checkout. If the total exceeds your order amount, the remaining balance will stay on the gift cards for future use.

What if I lose my gift card?

If you have the gift card code or purchase receipt, please contact our customer service team at gifts@foodhub.ug. We can help recover your gift card balance.

Can I get a refund for my gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable. However, they never expire and can be transferred to someone else if you're unable to use them.

Are there any restrictions on gift card usage?

Gift cards can be used for all FoodHub services including meal delivery, catering, and online ordering. They cannot be exchanged for cash.
